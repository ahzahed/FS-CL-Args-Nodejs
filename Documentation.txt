*** Packages I have used ***
--> yargs: to control command line. 
--> chalk: to make command output awesome.

*** To ADD note in 'notes.json' ***
node app.js add --title="Cocacola" --body="My favourite drink is Cocacola"

*** To Remove note in 'notes.json' ***
node app.js remove --title="Cocacola"

*** To View all note from 'notes.json' ***
node app.js list

*** To read specific note from 'notes.json' ***
node app.js read --title="Cocacola"
